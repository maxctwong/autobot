Autonomous Delivery Robot

The Autonomous Food Delivery Robot is a solution to food delivery problems in metropolitan areas. It helps lower the cost of food delivery services, making them more accessible to the general public. The goal of this project is to create an innovative solution to improve the current food delivery system.


The design objective of this project was to create a robot that can navigate indoor and outdoor areas autonomously. The robot was expected to avoid untraversable terrain as well as pedestrians and obstacles. The key features of the robot include the capability to move up to 15 pounds of cargo and scale more than 5 degrees of incline (the legal maximum limit of wheelchair ramps).


To verify the implementation of these designs, the robot was tested in multiple environments and various situations. Tests performed include static and dynamic obstacle avoidance, terrain detection, slope grade testing, and indoor and outdoor navigation. The testing results demonstrated that the robot successfully avoided all obstacles and navigated to its destination.


The Autonomous Food Delivery Robot is a user-friendly product that takes public health and safety into consideration. It ensures that to-go food deliveries are not contaminated and stolen. This product benefits all individuals socially and economically.
